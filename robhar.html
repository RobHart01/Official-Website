Who am I?

Originally from Osaka Japan, I came back to the U.S. during my 8th grade year. I later attended Oak Harbor High School graduating in 2015 earning my High School Diploma as well as my Associates Degree from Skagit Valley Community College as a Running Start Student. Being accepted at Washington State University, I graduated in 2018, earning my BA in Digital Technology and Culture. During my time at Washington State University, I found my love and passion for Coding and Design. Turning this curiosity into motion I began making websites and began understanding how the internet works and where my interests can take me. Changing my hobby, and free time into something more serious, I set my heart to becoming a full-blown full-stack web developer. Although I'm not exactly there yet, this website will show you my journey as a web developer and portray and reflect my skills as I grow in the industry and as an individual.
